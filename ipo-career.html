IPO Career Guide

What is an IPO?

An IPO (Initial Public Offering) is the process by which a private company becomes publicly traded by offering its shares to the public for the first time.

How to Start a Career in IPOs?

  • Learn about the stock market and IPO process.
  • Consider courses in finance, commerce, or business administration.
  • Look for internships or entry-level jobs at investment banks, brokerage firms, or financial consultancies.
  • Stay updated with IPO news and trends.

SME & All Categories

SME IPOs are for Small and Medium Enterprises. They have different eligibility and listing requirements compared to mainboard IPOs. Explore both to find your niche in the IPO ecosystem.

Career Roles

  • IPO Analyst
  • Investment Banker
  • Research Associate
  • Compliance Officer
  • Financial Advisor

For more, follow SEBI, NSE, BSE, and leading financial news portals.
